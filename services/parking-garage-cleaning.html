Parking Garage Cleaning Service

Each day, countless vehicles will drive through a parking garage. Every vehicle that drives in will be dragging mud, dust, and other grime along with it. They may even leak a bit of oil or leave tire marks. That’s just the cars. People will drop gum, splash about more mud, and even spill a drink or two. Some may even urinate (or worse). In short – parking garages get filthy. If you own a parking garage and you don’t invest in parking garage cleaning, you’re not giving off the best impression of your business.

Luckily for you – The Wash Squad are leaders in parking garage cleaning. With over a decade of experience, our team knows how to get your parking garage looking ship shape.

Why Parking Garage Cleaning is Important

If you own a parking garage, aesthetics are important. People won’t park their vehicles anywhere. They take immense pride in them, and if your parking garage is a mess, people will avoid your business. They’ll park elsewhere. It’ll cost you money.

If your parking garage is for the benefit of a business you own, you won’t be giving off the best first impression if you’ve got a filthy parking garage. Customers will be wary of dealing with you. They’ll believe that if you can’t keep your parking garage in good order, how can they trust the rest of your business?

That’s not all, either. Unclean parking garages require more maintenance. Dirt and grease stains left to fester on the surfaces will cause cracking and premature wear. Not to mention the fact that spilled oil can pose a tripping hazard, which can cause injury to your customers. That’ll be expensive to deal with.

In short – invest in parking garage cleaning. Your customers will be happier, you’ll be happier, and you’ll save money in the long run.

How The Wash Squad Can Help

Some parking garage owners will ask their staff to head out to the parking garage and scrub it down. This won’t work. While you’ll get it a little clean, it will be nowhere near enough to give you the benefits we mentioned. It’ll also take a very long time, even for the smallest of parking garages. If you want a proper parking garage cleaning, you’ll need specialist equipment and knowledge. This can be quite an investment. That’s why you should hire The Wash Squad. We’ll do everything for you, and we’ll make sure your parking garage is pristine!

With over a decade of experience in parking garage cleaning, The Wash Squad is equipped with everything needed to clean a parking garage. When we clean your parking garage, we’ll use our 6 high-powered hot water pressure cleaners, and soaps to blast away even the toughest of grime and stains. We can even remove stubborn gum that you may have struggled for a while to remove.

When you hire The Wash Squad to clean your garage, you’ll be hiring a team that has gone through enormous amounts of training. When we do a job, we always ensure we use the right equipment. We’ll use the right soap. We’ll use the right pressure on our pressure washers. Our goal is to get your parking garage as clean as possible without damaging the surfaces at all.

You’ll also be pleased to know that everything that The Wash Squad does will conform to EPA regulations. We only use eco-friendly soap and as little water as possible. This ensures your parking garage cleaning is not only safe but better for the environment.

Book The Wash Squad for Parking Garage Cleaning Today

There’s no job too big or small for The Wash Squad. Whether you need a quick cleaning of a small parking garage or a massive multi-story one, we have the equipment and staff to clean it for you at an affordable price. We have 6 hot water trailers ready to roll into your parking garage when you need us, each capable of pumping out 5 gallons of water per minute, getting your garage clean faster than ever.

If you want to book parking garage cleaning, give us a call. We’re happy to give you a quote for either a one-off clean or a regularly scheduled one. We even have a flexible schedule to get your garage cleaned on your terms (i.e. when it will cause the minimum disruption to your business).

So, pick up the phone and give us a call today! You’re one quick chat away from getting your parking garage looking great for both you and your customers!
