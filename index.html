State of the Art Pressure Washing

The Wash Squad comes equipped with professional grade pressure washing and environmental equipment. It doesn’t matter if the job is a simple house wash or a large parking garage, we have enough equipment to accommodate you. We put together one of the best power wash rigs in the country. It is capable of vacuuming mud, sludge dirt and grime from 200’ away. It then collects in a drum and after reaching maximum capacity it discharges it through our oil water separator and then filters down to 5 microns for reuse or discharge.
